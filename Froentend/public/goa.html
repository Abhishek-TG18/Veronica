Explore Goa

The sunny shores of Goa await you! Experience the charm of its beaches, the history of its forts and churches, and the vibrance of its culture. We recommend 5 days to explore the top attractions.

Your Goa Itinerary

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is a lively hub of adventure, scenic beauty, and vibrant nightlife, perfect for both relaxation and excitement.

Best Time: Morning on weekdays

Crowd Level: High

2

Aguada Fort

Aguada Fort is a historic Portuguese fortress in Goa, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and a glimpse into the region's colonial past.

Best Time: Early evening for sunset views

Crowd Level: Moderate

1

Mangueshi Temple

Temple in Goa is a serene and iconic shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its elegant architecture and spiritual ambiance.

Best Time: Early afternoon

Crowd Level: Low

3

Our Lady of Immaculate Conception Church

This church in Panaji is famed for its immaculate white façade and the striking zigzag staircase. Its Portuguese-Baroque and Goan architectural blend makes it one of the most unique churches in Goa.

Best Time: Sunset

Crowd Level: Low
